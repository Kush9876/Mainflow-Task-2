Thriller Books

Thriller books are characterized by suspense, excitement, and anticipation. They often involve dangerous situations, high stakes, and intense psychological tension that keeps readers on the edge of their seats.

Example Titles

  • Psychological Thriller: "Gone Girl" by Gillian Flynn, "The Girl on the Train" by Paula Hawkins
  • Legal Thriller: "The Firm" by John Grisham, "Presumed Innocent" by Scott Turow

Biography Books

Biographies are accounts of real people's lives, written by someone else. They provide insights into notable individuals, their achievements, struggles, and impact on society or their field of expertise.

Example Titles

  • Autobiography: "Long Walk to Freedom" by Nelson Mandela, "The Diary of a Young Girl" by Anne Frank
  • Memoir: "Educated" by Tara Westover, "Bossypants" by Tina Fey

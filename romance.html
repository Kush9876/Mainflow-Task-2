Romance Books

Romance books focus on romantic relationships and emotional connections between characters. They can vary from light-hearted and comedic to dramatic and poignant, exploring themes of love, attraction, and personal growth.

Example Titles

  • Contemporary Romance: "The Fault in Our Stars" by John Green, "Me Before You" by Jojo Moyes
  • Historical Romance: "Pride and Prejudice" by Jane Austen, "Outlander" series by Diana Gabaldon

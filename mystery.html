Mystery Books

Mystery books revolve around the investigation of a crime, puzzle, or enigma. They often feature detectives, amateur sleuths, or protagonists who uncover clues and solve mysteries through deduction, observation, and analysis.

Example Titles

  • Detective Fiction: "Sherlock Holmes" series by Arthur Conan Doyle, "The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo" by Stieg Larsson
  • Thriller: "Gone Girl" by Gillian Flynn, "The Da Vinci Code" by Dan Brown

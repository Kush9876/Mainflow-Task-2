Fiction Books

Fiction books encompass a wide range of imaginative storytelling, often exploring characters, settings, and plots that are created by the author's imagination rather than based on real events. Fiction can be further categorized into various subgenres like fantasy, science fiction, romance, historical fiction, and more.

Example Titles

  • Fantasy: "The Lord of the Rings" by J.R.R. Tolkien, "Harry Potter" series by J.K. Rowling
  • Science Fiction: "Dune" by Frank Herbert, "The Martian" by Andy Weir
  • Romance: "Pride and Prejudice" by Jane Austen, "The Notebook" by Nicholas Sparks

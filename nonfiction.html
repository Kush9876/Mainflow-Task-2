Non-fiction Books

Non-fiction books present factual information or real-life accounts, aiming to inform, educate, or analyze various subjects. They cover a wide range of topics including biographies, history, memoirs, self-help, true crime, and more.

Example Titles

  • Biography: "Steve Jobs" by Walter Isaacson, "Becoming" by Michelle Obama
  • History: "A People's History of the United States" by Howard Zinn, "The Rise and Fall of the Third Reich" by William L. Shirer
  • Self-help: "The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People" by Stephen R. Covey, "Atomic Habits" by James Clear

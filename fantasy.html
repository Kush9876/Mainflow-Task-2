Fantasy Books

Fantasy books transport readers to imaginary worlds filled with magic, mythical creatures, and epic quests. They often explore themes of heroism, adventure, and the battle between good and evil.

Example Titles

  • High Fantasy: "The Wheel of Time" series by Robert Jordan, "A Song of Ice and Fire" series by George R.R. Martin
  • Urban Fantasy: "Harry Dresden" series by Jim Butcher, "Neverwhere" by Neil Gaiman
